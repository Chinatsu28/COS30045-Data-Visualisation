Japanese migration analysis visualization

Migration patterns in Japan are a crucial gauge of societal and economic dynamics. Through a visual representation highlighting color saturation, users can easily grasp the intensity of migration in each prefecture. The varying hues indicate migration levels, offering a clear overview of population movement. This accessible visualization aims to inform decision-making by providing a refined understanding of Japan's evolving demographic landscape.

On the left is a bar chart that shows the number of people migrated to Japan and the line graph indicates the number of people migrated out of Japan from 2000 to 2021.
