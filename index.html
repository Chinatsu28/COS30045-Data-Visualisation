Choropleth Analysis

How to use this visualisation

For the Choropleth map:

  • Hover and click on a prefecture to open immigration and emigration tonador chart
  • After selecting the inflow/outflow side of the tonador chart, hovering on a prefecture will show its immigrants/emmigrants total numbers
  • Press the "Clear" button to reset the selection
  • Set the mouse in the Choropleth map and use scroll up/down to zoom in/out

For the tornado chart clicking on a rightside/leftside bars will:

  • Switch between inflow and outflow Choropleth map
  • Showing tree map based on immigration/emmigration number

For the Tree map:

  • Hover on each cells to view details information
  • Click on rightside/leftside bars of tornado chart to change the immigration/emmigration tree map
